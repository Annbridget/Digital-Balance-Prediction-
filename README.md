## Digital Balance Prediction App
Track. Predict. Improve. Master your digital wellness.

## Overview

Digital Balance is a Streamlit-based web app designed to help users understand their digital habits and lifestyle. It lets you track screen time, analyze device usage, and predict potential risks based on factors like sleep, stress, and productivity.

## Features
✅ Track personal and lifestyle metrics (device hours, sleep, stress, etc.) ✅ Get an instant digital wellness snapshot ✅ Predict your risk level using machine learning

## How It Works
Enter your personal info and daily habits (sleep, device time, etc.)
The app visualizes your digital lifestyle in real time
A predictive model estimates your digital balance score or risk level
Use the insights to adjust habits and improve well-being
